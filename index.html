Tic-tac-Yahooo (2 Players)

Welcome to the Mario vs. Bowser Tic-Tac-Yahoooo Showdown! In this exciting twist on the classic game, Mario and Bowser face off for ultimate control over the Mushroom Kingdom!

Play!

How to Play:

  1. Players:
    • Player 1 is represented by Mario
    • Player 2 is represented by Bowser
  2. Objective:

    The goal is to get three in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally using your character (Mario or Bowser). The first player to achieve this wins the game!

  3. Game Board:

    The Tic-Tac-Toe board is a 3x3 grid, just like the traditional version. However, instead of using circles (O) and crosses (X), Player 1 will place Mario's image in a square, and Player 2 will place Bowser's image in their chosen square.

  4. Turn Sequence:
    • Player 1 (Mario) goes first and selects a square to place Mario's image.
    • Player 2 (Bowser) then chooses a different square to place Bowser's image.
    • The game alternates between players until one player gets three of their images in a row or the board is full.
  5. Winning the Game:
    • If a player gets three Mario or three Bowser images in a straight line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal), they win!
    • If all squares are filled and no one has three in a row, it's a draw.

Mario Shadow Quest

Welcome to Mario Shadow Quest, where you step into the Mushroom Kingdom and put your Mario knowledge to the test! Can you recognize your favorite characters from just their shadow?

Play!

How to Play:

  1. Objective:

    As you adventure through this game, you'll see mysterious silhouettes of Mario and his pals! But here's the twist - you only get two options to guess who it is. Choose wisely and collect as many points as you can before the time runs out.

  2. The Clock's Ticking!

    You've got just 10 seconds per question! Time flies faster than a Super Star, so make your guess quick or the Koopa Troopas will catch up with you!

  3. Scoring:
    • Correct answers? You score a big 1-Up!
    • Wrong answers? Oh no, you'll take a hit like falling into a pit!
  4. Winning the Game:

    Rack up as many correct answers as you can before the time runs out! Show Bowser who's boss by setting a high score. Can you become the Mushroom Kingdom's shadow-guessing champion?
